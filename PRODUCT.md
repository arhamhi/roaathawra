# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners and design-conscious buyers in Pakistan who want made-to-order furniture without a showroom checkout flow. They browse visually, compare pieces quickly, then message Roaathawra on Instagram or WhatsApp to customize size, finish, and timeline.

## Product Purpose

Roaathawra is a static brand and catalog site for a woman-led furniture studio. Success means visitors understand the craft standard, see real product photography immediately, find a piece they like, and start a DM or WhatsApp order with the product name already in context.

## Brand Personality

Refined, warm, architectural. The brand should feel handcrafted and considered, with enough editorial confidence to justify premium made-to-order pricing, but without sounding like a generic luxury showroom.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic ecommerce storefront patterns, oversized fake luxury claims, beige template sameness, empty image placeholders, checkout-first language, and AI-looking card grids that hide the actual furniture. Do not make the site feel like a SaaS landing page or a Shopify theme with the cart removed.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the room and the object, not explanation.
- Keep ordering human: browse, choose, message, customize.
- Treat price transparency as trust, not discounting.
- Preserve the quiet craft tone while making the catalog easy to scan.
- Use real media and responsive optimization as part of the brand experience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for text contrast, semantic structure, keyboard navigation, focus visibility, descriptive alt text, and reduced-motion support. Mobile touch targets should be comfortable for one-handed browsing.
