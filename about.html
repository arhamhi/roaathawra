About Us

High Design Meets Architectural Rigour.

In a market flooded with overpriced "luxury" showrooms and low-quality replicas, finding furniture that actually makes sense is harder than it should be. Most shops sell you a look. We sell you an intelligently crafted space.

The Revolution of Imagination

Roaathawra was born from something deeply personal. The name brings together Roaa, meaning Imagination, and Thawra, my mother's name, meaning Revolution. It is a tribute to her strength and vision, and together it represents a powerful idea: the Revolution of Imagination.

Founded during the quiet uncertainty of the COVID era, Roaathawra began with thoughtfully crafted study tables designed for homes adapting to a new way of living. What started as a small, heartfelt initiative soon evolved into a contemporary furniture brand defined by intention, refinement, and timeless minimalism.

Guided by the philosophy of "less is more," we create pieces that embody quiet luxury: clean lines, balanced proportions, and enduring craftsmanship. Every design is carefully produced in Pakistan by skilled artisans who bring precision and care to every detail.

From statement dining tables to tailored sofas and refined workspaces, our collections are designed to elevate everyday living.

At Roaathawra, we do not simply create furniture. We create spaces shaped by imagination and inspired by legacy.

Welcome to the revolution.

"Less is More" is a Choice, Not a Shortcut

Minimalism is often used as an excuse for "simple" work. For us, it is a performance standard. Our design philosophy follows the principle that elegance comes from precision. We create furniture that stands out through its silence: pieces that blend seamlessly into your home while providing the structural integrity required for daily use.

Material Intelligence

We don't play the "solid wood" guessing game. We use the right materials for the right jobs. Whether it's the rich grain of Sheesham and Mahogany for heirloom pieces, or high-density engineered wood for the sharp, matte finishes that define contemporary design, every choice is intentional. We use materials that stand up to the Pakistani climate without warping, cracking, or failing you in two years.

The Backbone of the Brand

Roaathawra is a woman-led company, but we are a team-powered operation. Our carpenters, upholsterers, painters, and fabricators are the hardest-working people in Pakistan. They are the backbone of our brand. When you buy from us, you aren't just getting a bed. You are supporting an eco-friendly, 100% Pakistani ecosystem that treats labour with respect and materials with care.

My aim is simple: I want to take this brand international, to show the world that Pakistani craftsmanship, when led by architectural rigour, is second to none.
