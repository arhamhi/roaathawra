About Roaathawra

High Design Meets Architectural Rigor.

In a market flooded with overpriced "luxury" showrooms and low-quality replicas, furniture that actually makes sense is harder to find than it should be. Most shops sell you a look. We sell you an intelligently crafted space.

The Revolution of Imagination.

Roaathawra was born from something deeply personal. Roaa means imagination; Thawra, my mother's name, means revolution. Together, they became the idea this brand is built on.

Founded in the quiet uncertainty of the COVID era, Roaathawra began with thoughtfully crafted study tables for homes adapting to a new way of living. What started as a small, heartfelt initiative soon grew into a contemporary furniture brand defined by intention, refinement and timeless minimalism, every design produced in Pakistan by skilled artisans who bring precision and care to every detail.

Our Standard

"Less is More" is a choice, not a shortcut.

Minimalism is often an excuse for simple work. For us it's a performance standard: elegance comes from precision. We make pieces that stand out through their silence and blend seamlessly into your home, while holding the structural integrity daily life demands.

Material Intelligence

The right material for the right job.

We don't play the "solid wood" guessing game. Rich Sheesham and Mahogany for heirloom pieces; high-density engineered wood for sharp, matte contemporary finishes. Every choice is intentional, chosen to stand up to the Pakistani climate without warping, cracking or failing you in two years.

The Backbone of the Brand

A woman-led company. A team-powered operation.

Our carpenters, upholsterers, painters and fabricators are some of the hardest-working hands in Pakistan. They are the backbone of the brand. When you buy from Roaathawra, you support real craftsmanship and the people behind it.
